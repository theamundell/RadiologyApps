Case 4.5

What pathological finding do these radiographs of different patients demonstrate? (Caveat: b is a normal reference)

Diagnosis

  • The finding is Eburnation (arrows)
  • a) magnified AP subchondral region of the hip joint demonstrating increased subchondral sclerosis on both sides of the joint
  • b) normal example for reference
  • c) similar sclerosis secondary to degenerative disease of the medial tibiofemoral joint space

Reference
