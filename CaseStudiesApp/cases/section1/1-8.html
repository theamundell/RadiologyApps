Diagnosis: Psoriatic Arthritis

  • DIP predominance with erosive change, and lack of periarticular osteopenia, subchondral sclerosis or osteophytosis.
  • Pencil-in-cup deformity at the left 3rd DIP (arrow)
  • Subtle new bone formation
