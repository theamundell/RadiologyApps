Case 1.11
Review the following images. What is the diagnosis?

Diagnosis: Spondyloarthropathy

  • Sagittal MRI mid-cervical spine on T2 (left) and T1 (right).
  • There is multilevel syndesmophyte formation, both anteriorly (arrow) and posteriorly.
  • In addition there is anterior corner post-inflammatory fat accentuation, and focal high signal intensity on T1 and T2, most pronounced anteriorly at C2-3.
