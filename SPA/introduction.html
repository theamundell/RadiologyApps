Unravelling Spondyloarthropathy

Essential Imaging In Rheumatology

Unravelling is an appropriate term in many ways for this App based on
“Axial Spondyloarthropathy”. SpA has experienced an almost
unprecedented onslaught of research and management guidelines over
the last decade with the advent of new treatments in the form of anti-TNFs.
Unravelling the disease process, including the clinical and imaging
features can be unnerving at best. We have sought to provide a solid
foundation of imaging findings in this disease, its variable appearance and
its mimickers in a clear and concise fashion.

App Overview

  • The App is divided into seven sections, each with clearly defined goals.
  • Section 1 provides a brief overview of axial spondyloarthropathy.
  • Section 2 reviews normal imaging anatomy of both the sacroiliac joints and the spine
  • Section 3 details the various imaging findings in the sacroiliac joints and spine
  • Section 4 provides an imaging algorithm
  • Section 5 discusses the diagnosis of axial spondyloarthropathy
  • Section 6 reviews the common alternate pathologies and imaging appearances
  • Section 7 provides “real-life” clinical cases, approach and diagnosis

How To Guide

  • Once introduction is complete an “Index” button will appear to bring you to the index page
  • Choose the section you would like to enter first and tap that button. We have organized the index in a sequential pattern that we felt would build upon each preceding section but you are free to navigate.
  • Once a section is open one can tap the arrows on the right or left side of the page to proceed to the next or previous page or swipe in the appropriate direction.
  • At the completion of each section the main index page reappears for the next selection.
  • The index button is always present in the top left corner and can be accessed at any time to select a different section of the App
